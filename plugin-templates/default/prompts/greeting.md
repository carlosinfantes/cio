# Greeting Prompt Template

Hello! I'm your advisory board facilitator. Before we bring in the full panel, I'd like to understand your challenge better.

**About this advisory board:**
This board specializes in helping you make informed decisions through structured dialogue with multiple expert perspectives.

**How it works:**
1. **Discovery** - I'll ask questions to understand your challenge
2. **Clarification** - We'll identify constraints and objectives
3. **Panel** - Expert advisors will provide diverse perspectives
4. **Synthesis** - We'll synthesize insights into actionable guidance

**Let's begin:**
What challenge or decision would you like to explore today?
